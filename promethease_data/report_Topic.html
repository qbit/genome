rs7171755 is a SNP near the synaptic cell adhesion glycoprotein-encoding NPTN gene. A study of ~1,500 adolescents determined that the rs7171755(A) allele is associated with a slightly thinner left hemisphere cortex and also a slight decrease in verbal and nonverbal intelligence (IQ). The differences were really quite slight; each copy of the risk allele decreased cortical thickness by 0.7%, and decreased verbal and nonverbal IQ by 1.8 and 1.4 points, respectively.
